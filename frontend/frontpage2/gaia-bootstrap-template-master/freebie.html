About Us

✻

Sattva, is a Sanskrit word that means good, essence and is used to refer to nature, as well.
Sattva was built to automatically take images, created or owned by the park and convert them into art. We converted these images into NFT and put it on sale on the OpenSea platform.

Self-Sovereign

We help in capturing the value generated by a park and harnessing that value. We help to enable nature to govern itself for its sustainability and ours as well.

Park Optimisation

We demonstrate a way in which the park can generate income and optimize itself to attract more crowds. We help the park to showcase its beauty to the world.

Revenue Utilisation

Sattva provides suggestions for the trees that can be planted, by considering resource availability, aesthetic aspect, biodiversity and the planting cost
